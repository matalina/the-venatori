The Venatori



The Dawning

Most kids my age are graduating college right about now. Me, I'm hunting dragons — or I was until my boss called me home to help figure out who was hunting us in our own house. I'd been sent away so I didn't have to deal with the politics of what it meant to be Venatori — to be free for just a little while. But the moment I walk in the door that world surrounds me and the one little hope I find in all of it is unavailable for the taking. But I have faith there is always a way and I will find it — even if it kills me.
